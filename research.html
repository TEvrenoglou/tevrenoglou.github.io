Research

The research profile of Dr. Theodoros Evrenoglou

Scientific Interests

My research focuses on advancing statistical methodologies in meta-analysis, particularly addressing challenges with sparse data in network meta-analysis. I develop and validate novel statistical approaches to improve evidence synthesis in medical research.

Research Areas

  • Network Meta-Analysis
  • Bayesian Statistics
  • Sparse Data Methodologies
  • Evidence Synthesis
  • Clinical Trial Methodology

Current Projects

I am currently leading a DFG-funded project on "Advanced Methods for Handling Sparse Data in Network Meta-Analysis" which aims to develop robust statistical techniques for situations with limited data availability.
