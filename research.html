Scientific interests

I am a Senior Researcher and the group leader of the Meta-Analysis group at the Institute of Medical Biometry and Statistics, part of the Faculty of Medicine and Medical Center at the University of Freiburg in Germany. In addition, I am the Principal Investigator of a research grant funded by the German Research Foundation (Deutsche Forschungsgemeinschaft: DFG).

I obtained a BSc in Mathematics and an MSc in Statistics and Operational Research from the Department of Mathematics at the University of Ioannina in 2017 and 2019, respectively. I was awarded a PhD in Biostatistics in 2022 from Université Paris Cité and my thesis titled "Dealing with Sparse Data in Network Meta-Analysis".
